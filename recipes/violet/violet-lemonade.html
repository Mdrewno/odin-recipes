Fresh Violet Lemonade

On a sunny spring day it is a great opportunity to get outside and find a field of violets to forage for fresh lemonade. Adding violets to lemonade brings in a slight floral flavor and a beautiful light purple tint. Poured over ice, this drink makes a perfect springtime refreshment.

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces fresh violet flowers (no leaves or stems)
  • 1 cup of honey
  • 1 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 quart of water
  • ice

Steps

  1. Steep violet flowers in 1-quart of boiled water for about 15 minutes.
  2. Strain the mixture, pouring the violet-infused water into a 2-quart pitcher.
  3. Stir in 1 cup of honey.
  4. Stir in 1 cup of fresh squeezed lemon juice.
  5. Added in enough water to fill up the pitcher.
  6. When ready to serve pour over a glass of ice and garnish with lemon slices and/or violet blossoms.
